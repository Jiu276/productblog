Detailed Review

Design & Build: iPhone 15 Pro features a titanium frame design, reducing weight by approximately 19g compared to stainless steel, with the device weighing only 187g. Titanium offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio but requires careful handling due to surface sensitivity. The Action Button replaces the silent switch, offering customizable functions that significantly enhance usability.

Performance: A17 Pro uses 3nm process technology with 10% CPU performance improvement and 20% GPU performance boost. It runs Genshin Impact at maximum settings stably at 60fps, and Honor of Kings at 120fps mode with excellent thermal management. Daily app switching is smooth with outstanding multitasking capabilities.

Photography Experience: Main camera upgraded to 48MP with 2x zoom capability, equivalent to 48mm focal length ideal for portrait photography. Night mode shows excellent noise control with accurate color reproduction. Ultra-wide lens has good distortion control, telephoto supports 3x optical zoom, delivering professional-grade photography experience.

Battery & Charging: Battery capacity approximately 3274mAh, lasting a full day with heavy usage. Supports 27W wired fast charging and 15W MagSafe wireless charging, with charging speeds in the mid-range for flagship phones. USB-C connector supports data transfer and external display connection, greatly improving practicality.
